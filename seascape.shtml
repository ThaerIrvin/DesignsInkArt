Welcome to the Seascape and Shells Gallery.

Both contemporary and more traditional seascapes are the subject of this gallery as well as all things seashore. Originals are painted on both smooth 90# and textural 140# watercolor papers using color washes and sketchy watercolor lines to help define the subject while still allowing your imagination to be transported. The newest series of mixed media waves is composed of textural gesso and watercolor, still abstract in nature. Many of these originals may also be shown in other galleries and some are also available as greeting cards.

Contact me additional for information.
