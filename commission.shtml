For over 25 years Designs Ink, Chris Paschke, has been a calligraphic designer, fine art calligrapher, and artist who has designed everything from logos to original artworks. She works any size, any color, commercial or personal, framed or unframed, as a single or series. Clients with special requests are encouraged to contact her for information at info@designsinkart.com

Licensing opportunities are available for many of the original art pieces found on this site. Past licensees include Antioch Publishing, Croscill, Intercontinental Art, Peter Pauper Press, New View, and Wallquest, and are seen all over the globe. To discuss licensing and image availability email the above link, or call Chris at Designs Ink directly.

Larson-Juhl

Small squares were written
with stylized Asian characters
designed to run off the corners.

They were then framed and arranged
into a patchwork grid for a
Larson-Juhl booth design.

JCM Corporation

A series of four 48"x96" layered
walnut ink panels were designed
for their new corporate lobby.

The Japanese characters Love, Peace,
Harmony and Prosperity were written
as 24" resist forms
overlaid with calligraphic sentiments.

Callimats

Callimats are the integration
of fine art calligraphy
and customized framing,
where the two combine to create
a unified piece of fine art.

Common commissions have included
special quotations,
lyrics, and vows as thank yous
and anniversary gifts.
