Larson-Juhl

These 14"x18" pieces were commissioned by Larson-Juhl as originals to showcase the release of their new mouldings.
Each piece incorporates the name of the moulding and one or two words describing the profile. Textural gesso freely replicates the sculpture or carving of the wood.
All of these pieces were mixed media of Swiss walnut ink, resist, gesso and graphite, on Arches 260# HP white watercolor paper.
