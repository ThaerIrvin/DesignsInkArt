Welcome to the Cityscape Gallery.

Contemporary and abstracted cityscapes are the subject of this gallery. Originals are painted on textural 140# watercolor papers using color washes and sketchy watercolor lines to help define the subject while still allowing your imagination fill in the city imagery. They have been created in sets of vertical and horizontal presentations including 10"x30" as 1:3 format. There are a few sets that are mixed media black ink, heavy black acrylic and/or gold leaf.

Many of these originals may also be shown in other galleries and some are also available as greeting cards.Contact us for additional information.
