Silk Scroll Tranquility

10"x30"

Authentic Chinese raw silk is the backing for this contemporary series of four. Each features western calligraphy written in white gouache on black paper and a Chinese character. A textural block simulates the Chinese chop.

All layers, trim, accent, character and calligraphy are not yet permanently mounted.

One of a set of four: Harmony, Happiness, Tranquility and Tao.
