Welcome to the Nudes and Figurative Gallery.

The figurative original art in this gallery includes everything from monoline to rich walnut ink washes over textured gesso, creating both male and female nudes. All are abstracted and interpretive having a mystery about them, with the newest series being even beyond single line art to minimalism which is currently very on trend in home decor.

Many are painted as series allowing for horizontal and vertical set arrangements of three, four or five. They are also available as greetings cards upon request.
