Welcome to the Floral Gallery.

The original flowers in this gallery are an eclectic collection of everything from minimalist traditional sumi-e painting to contemporary abstracts and mixed media. Flowers of all types are painted on hard surface, watercolor, and mulberry rice papers while collages include color washes on heavily textured gesso borders and in some cases even fabrics. The newest featured flowers are watercolor, gouache and ink composites in portrait layout, very contemporary, some even feature gold leaf, silver leaf and copper leaf.
