Welcome to the Abstract and Contemporary Gallery.

The original art in this gallery features abstract and contemporary themes featuring layers of watercolor, gouache, ink, and gold leaf as described in each individual description. Images may resemble landscapes, seascapes, cityscapes or simply be abstracted brush strokes as expressions of shapes and colors. Though any one original creates a complete look and may be displayed alone, many originals have been created in pairs, sets of three or four, or a series of ten or twelve. This allows for images to be clustered and displayed as series that are vertically aligned, horizontally aligned, as a four square or more to better fill a large wall or cathedral ceiling.

For other more specific interprtations--though often still abstractions--visit the individual themed galleries on this site.
