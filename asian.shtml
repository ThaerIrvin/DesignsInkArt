Welcome to the Asian Gallery.

The original art in this gallery include Asian calligraphy, sumi-e painting, and collage using Asian inspired themes. A wide variety of characters, bamboo, birds and animals all find their way into both contemporary and traditional formats. They have been created on an assortment of papers and accent backings and many are mixed media. Many of these have been created to be displayed in sets of three vertical or horizontal, four square, or nine as three rows of three.
