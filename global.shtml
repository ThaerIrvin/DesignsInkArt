Welcome to the Global Gallery.

Global art is that which relates to the entire world. The original art in this gallery includes world maps, gloabal fashion, masks and symbols created with watercolor, gouache, ink, gesso and gold leaf as mixed media and pure mediums on an assortment of papers. The masks have all been based on authentic African and island masks, the Buddhas on existing statuary, and Yoga references and symbols have all been researched and created with artistic contemporary interpretation. Global gallery is not the only reference to global art...also visit Asian, Sumi, and Characters Galleries for Asian influenced art by Chris Paschke.

Square images are also available as notecards and 8"x 8" framed prints and most have been created as sets of four, six, or eight.

Click on any small image to view an enlargement with details and price.
