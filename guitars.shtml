Welcome to the Guitars Gallery.

The originals in this gallery are currently non-representational essences of guitars. They are watercolor and gouache on textural 140# cold press, handmade watercolor paper, all currently 5"x 10" in size. Many are painted as sets of two, three or four in similar colors and there are two fairly distinct style versions: one rather etherial and airly painted with pastel coloring--found in the first five rows, the other bolder painted in a more primary color palette.

Contact me additional for information or licensing inquiries.
