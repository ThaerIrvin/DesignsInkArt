Welcome to the Graphics and Patterns Gallery.

The original art in this gallery include black and white graphic designs that could easily be converted into repetetive patterns for softgoods, wallpaper and such. Many were created as special assignments and as pattern options to accompany other art themes including wine, florals and backgrounds for thoughtful statements. Some of these may be also listed in the Licensing Gallery.
