Welcome to the Wine Gallery.

The images in this gallery are all related to the consumption and enjoyment of wine, champagne and the enjoyment of friends and spirits. The original art is an eclectic collection of everything from traditional still life to contemporary abstract bottle and wine vignettes. The Gilded Wine and Gilded Champagne series showcase gold leaf and ink on smooth white paper crisp and clean like the perfect wine. Further down the page are layered calligraphic tiles in rich walnut ink created with resist featuring words and sentiment tthat go along with any great glass of wine. Other pieces reflect the use of the right glass for the right wine. Enjoy and abide often.

Click on any small image to view an enlargement with details, options and pricing.
