Welcome to the Pots and Vessels Gallery.

The original art in this gallery represents an assortment of interpretive pot images as an assortment of potted plants and cut flowers in pots, vases, bottles and containers; staged bonsai trees; traditional Asian influenced heavy line ink paintings; and contemporary flat non-dimensional still life vessels, bottles and vases. All have the typical Paschke interpretation and flair.
