Welcome to the Framed Originals Gallery.

Custom professional picture framing is available for all of her art. If from out-of-town originals are easily shipped for framing with your local professional. Framing is the best way to enhance and protect your new art and should be taken very seriously using only the highest quality acid free and neutral materials, UV or museum glass and proper hanging hardware. Never display your original art in direct sunlight even if it only gets that light for a short duration each day.

The Framed pieces shown here are currently hanging in Designs Ink Gallery and are abvailable for viewing by appointment or for immediate delivery, unless marked as sold.
