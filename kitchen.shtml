Welcome to the Kitchen Gallery.

The original art in this gallery began as an assortment of fruits and vegetables on mulberry paper, that would be great additions to a small space in any kitchen. Over the years it has grown to add teapots and small calligraphic pieces on smooth white paper and most recently bold ink images on white. Time change and so does the trend in art. Keeping up with new kitchen designing the art must follow suit.

Click on any small image to view an enlargement with details and price.
