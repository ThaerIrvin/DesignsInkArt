Welcome to Designs Ink's gallery of Chris Paschke open edition reproductions--published by Wild Apple Graphics--going back to 1998. Designs Ink is the only source for Chris Paschke out-of-print images. Many of the florals, characters and bamboo images are available as pairs or sets of three, four or more. All images are available as unframed prints or are sold framed on other internet sites. This is the only internet site that allows for specialty custom framing to meet per your specific needs while still enhancing the art. Keep in mind a fine frame design can enhance or destroy the look of any print.

The thumbnails below are just a sampler of the image with print size and title listed below each for reference.
Click the thumbnail to see an enlargement of the image with full details.
Retail prices are for an unframed reproduction, and many are available as a series of four or six and in variable sizes. Shipping is FOB Tehachapi CA, with custom framing available upon request.

Enjoy your visit and email us if you have any questions.
