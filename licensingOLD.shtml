Welcome to the Licensing Gallery.

This is a commercial gallery designed for review and inspiration by potential licensors. The original art represents images designed for licensed use in prototypes to be converted into finished retail products--other than two-dimensional open edition prints--for kitchen, cooking, bath, bedroom, dining, children, holidays, and everyday uses as towels, pot holders, placemats, dishes, tablecloths, napkins, stationery, notepads, notecards, paper goods, linens, clothing and assorted accessories. Additional backgrounds and patterns may be created to meet products development needs. New versions of any grouping may be available and/or created upon serious request, time permitting. At the bottom of this page are a series of unfinished backgrounds designed to be matched with featured images or product development as desired.

For licensing requests and additional information contact Chris directly at 661-821-2188 or email me at info@designsinkart.com
