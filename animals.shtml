Welcome to the Animal Gallery.

The original animal art currently on display in this gallery include cats, kittens, bunnies, fish, and roosters painted on assorted handmade Asian rice papers as well as more traditional watercolor paintng on 140# cold pressed textural and hot pressed smooth papers. Sumi inks and watercolors from China and Japan, walnut ink granules from Switzerland, European watercolors and opaque gouache are my mediums of choice. Most of the Asian influenced originals are signed using my Chinese name Mei Giu Pin and or my stacked CAP initials in ink or graphite accented by my personalized carved chop and vermillion ink. Since I favor smaller formats for my sumi ink paintings I most often create sets or groupings that may easily be arranged horizontally or vertically to accommodate any design space.

These original paintings are available framed or unframed, as limited editions, and as greeting cards. Contact me for additional information. They are also available for licensing.
