Welcome to the Bamboo Gallery.

Bamboo of all kinds abound here. They have been painted on rag paper and mulberry rice papers using black and gray sumi inks, rich brown walnut inks and Chinese colors. The original leaf art in this gallery are an eclectic collection of everything from minimalist traditional sumi-e painting to contemporary abstract. The abstractions are often mixed media collaged with an assortment of imagery and often in set or series.

Many of the bamboo and scroll panels featured here may also have been posted in the Asian, Leaf, and Licensing Galleries.

Click on any small image to view an enlargement with details and price. If no price is listed email for additional information, and as with all of Chris Paschke originals they are available unframed or framed. Call or email for details. info@designsinkart.com
